What Is a Mistrial?

A mistrial occurs when a trial is invalidated due to an error or serious issue that prevents a conclusion. This could be due to a hung jury, procedural error, or other significant disruptions. Understanding the mistrial meaning in the context of your case is crucial, as it can impact the strategy moving forward. Learn more about trial proceedings at RRK Law.
