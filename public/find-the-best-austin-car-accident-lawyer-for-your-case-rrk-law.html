    Understanding the Causes of Car Accidents in Austin

    In Austin, car accidents are often caused by distracted driving, reckless behavior while behind the wheel, and impaired driving. It is important to be aware of these potential hazards in order to avoid becoming involved in an accident yourself or being part of another's misfortune.

    Distracted Driving

    Driving while not paying full attention to the road can have severe repercussions and is one of the leading causes of vehicle collisions in Austin.     Speeding and Reckless Driving

    Driving too quickly and in an irresponsible manner carries huge consequences such as a lack of vehicle control, less efficient airbag protection, longer stopping distance, and heightened accident probability.     It is important to stick to posted speed limits at all times while practicing safe habits behind the wheel in order to avert accidents and keep everyone on roadways secure.

    The dangers of driving while impaired by drugs or alcohol can never be overstated.
