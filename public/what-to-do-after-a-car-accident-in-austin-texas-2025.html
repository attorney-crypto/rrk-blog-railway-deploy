Understanding Texas's Modified Comparative Fault System

What to do after car accident Texas law requires understanding includes knowing your state's unique fault system. Texas follows a 51% bar rule for personal injury claims under Civil Practice and Remedies Code Section 33.001. Houston personal injury attorneys and Dallas criminal defense lawyers emphasize that if you're 50% or less at fault, you can recover damages reduced by your fault percentage. However, bearing 51% or more fault completely bars recovery.

This rule applies to all tort-based claims including car accidents, assault cases, and other personal injury matters, making immediate documentation of the accident scene crucial. Texas car accident lawyers recommend photographing vehicle positions, obtaining witness statements, and preserving evidence before vehicles are moved.
