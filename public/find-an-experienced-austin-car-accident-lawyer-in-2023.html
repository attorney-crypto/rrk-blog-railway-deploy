How much do lawyers take from car accident settlements in Texas?

In Texas, personal injury lawyers typically receive a fee of 25-40% from the finalized settlement. This is usually deducted before any amount is disbursed to the injured individual. Thus, their fees are taken out of what’s awarded in such cases.

Can you sue for a car accident in Texas?

In the state of Texas, you can bring a lawsuit against the person who caused your accident if they are found to have been at fault. It is not possible to seek damages from their insurance company. Only an individual may face liability for any resulting harm.
