What are my rights if I have been in a bicycle accident in Texas?

📅 January 25, 2026
✍️ Law Offices of RRK, LLC

It depends on the underlying facts surrounding your case. The law provides certain protections and benefits which can help those injured in these accidents seek justice.

The first thing to keep in mind is that the laws governing bicycle accidents are similar to those for any other type of vehicle accident. If someone else caused or contributed to the accident, they may be liable for any damages incurred as a result.
