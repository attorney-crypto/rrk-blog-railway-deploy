What does personal injury mean in legal terms?

Personal injury is a term often used to describe instances where someone has been hurt due to the negligence of another party. It doesn’t mean that the other person or entity was intentionally trying to cause harm, but rather they were careless in regard to safety considerations. This can be attributed as an act not arising from ill-will towards others and without any malicious intent by those accused of wrongdoing in these cases.
