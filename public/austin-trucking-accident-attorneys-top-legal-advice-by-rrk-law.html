Austin's trucking regulations, aligning with both Texas and federal laws, govern various aspects of the trucking industry. At RRK Law, we have a deep understanding of these regulations, equipping us to navigate the legal system effectively on your behalf.

What to Do After a Trucking Accident

The moments following a trucking accident are critical. Here are the steps we recommend:

  • Prioritize Safety: Ensure your and others' safety first. Move to a safe location if possible.
  • Notify Authorities: Report the accident immediately to the Austin Police Department.
  • Document Everything: Gather as much evidence as possible – photos of the scene, witness contact information, and any relevant details.
  • Seek Medical Attention: Even if injuries seem minor, it's crucial to get a medical evaluation.
  • Consult with RRK Law: Before engaging with insurance companies, seek legal advice to understand your rights and options.
